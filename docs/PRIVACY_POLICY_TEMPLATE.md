
# BioLoop Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

BioLoop ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your health data when you use the BioLoop mobile application (the "App").

## Data We Access

BioLoop accesses the following health data from Apple HealthKit:

- Heart rate samples
- Resting heart rate
- Heart rate variability (HRV SDNN)
- VO2 max
- Workout sessions
- Sleep analysis
- Body mass
- Height
- Date of birth

## How We Use Your Data

Your health data is used exclusively to:

1. Calculate your daily Performance Index
2. Calculate your biological age (BioAge)
3. Generate personalized health insights
4. Display trends and progress over time

**All calculations are performed locally on your device. Your health data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.**

## Data Storage

- All health data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (AES-256)
- We do not sync your data to iCloud or any cloud service
- We do not have access to your health data
- Your data remains on your device at all times

## Data Collection

We do NOT collect:

- Personal information
- Health data
- Usage analytics
- Device information
- Location data
- Crash reports

BioLoop is designed with privacy-first principles. We have no servers, no databases, and no way to access your data.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

1. Opening the Profile tab
2. Tapping "Delete All Data"
3. Confirming the deletion

This will permanently remove all BioLoop data from your device. This action cannot be undone.

## Third-Party Services

BioLoop does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not share your data with anyone.

## Subscription Data

If you purchase a subscription:

- Payment is processed by Apple through the App Store
- We do not have access to your payment information
- Apple's privacy policy applies to payment processing
- Subscription status is stored locally on your device

## Children's Privacy

BioLoop is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## HealthKit Data

BioLoop uses Apple HealthKit to access your health data. By granting HealthKit permissions, you allow BioLoop to read the specified health data types. You can revoke these permissions at any time in the iOS Health app settings.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your data (stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data (via the Profile tab)
- Revoke HealthKit permissions (via iOS Health app)
- Request information about our data practices

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- Email: privacy@bioloop.app
- Website: https://bioloop.app/privacy

## Legal Compliance

BioLoop complies with:

- Apple's HealthKit Review Guidelines
- iOS App Store Review Guidelines
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
